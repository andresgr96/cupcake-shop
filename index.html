Timothys signature Cupcake

Timothys signature Cupcake is the lemongrass french JaggerBomb salty cupcake. This cupcake was created by our founder Andrew Timothy in 1946 after spending a whole year in the Russian winter with nothing more the lemon, Jaggermeister and vanilla.
